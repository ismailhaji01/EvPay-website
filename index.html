Got Questions? We Have Got Answers

Can I start without a developer?+
Yes. You can use Payment Links and digital invoices to go live instantly without coding. EVPay provides a simple dashboard where you can track transactions, monitor payments, and manage your business in real time.
How fast do I receive funds?+
Settlement times depend on the payment channel used. However, you will always have real-time visibility of transaction status and settlement updates through your dashboard.
Can we test before going live?+
Yes. EVPay provides a sandbox environment where you can simulate transactions, test payment flows, run sample batches, and handle return scenarios safely before processing real payments.
How do you handle failed or returned payments?+
Failed or returned payments are communicated using return codes and webhook notifications. This allows your system to automatically retry transactions or offer alternative payment options.
Do you support approvals for large transactions?+
Yes. EVPay supports multi-step approval workflows with configurable thresholds and alerts, ensuring secure handling of high-value or bulk transactions.
